Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an IPTV profile?

Open the app, tap the "+" button, select your connection type (M3U URL or Xtream Codes), enter your credentials, and save. Your IPTV provider should give you the necessary connection details.

Is Quantum IPTV Player free?

Yes, the app is completely free to download and use. However, you need your own IPTV subscription from a service provider.

Do you provide IPTV services or content?

No, Quantum IPTV Player is a media player only. We do not provide, sell, or distribute any IPTV services, subscriptions, or content. You must obtain these from legitimate IPTV service providers.

Why isn't my playlist loading?

Common issues include: incorrect URL, expired subscription, server downtime, or network connectivity problems. Verify your credentials with your IPTV provider and check your internet connection.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your credentials and profile information are stored locally on your device using AES-256 encryption. We do not store your data on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can install the app on multiple devices. However, your IPTV provider may limit the number of simultaneous connections. Check with your provider for their policy.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Clear All Data in the app, or simply uninstall the application. All locally stored data will be removed.

The app crashed. What should I do?

We automatically receive crash reports. Please email us at quantumiptv.dev@gmail.com with details about what you were doing when the crash occurred. We'll investigate and fix the issue in the next update.
